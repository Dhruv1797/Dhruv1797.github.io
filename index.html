Privacy Policy for Wetime App

Last Updated: 29/08/2024

Welcome to Wetime! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Collected during OTP authentication.
  • Profile Data: Username, date of birth, gender, and profile image provided during profile creation.
  • Uploaded Content: Images and descriptions uploaded by you within the app.

1.2. Permissions

  • Camera: To capture and upload profile images or content within the app.
  • Contacts: For potential features that allow interaction with your contacts.
  • Microphone: For audio features within the app.
  • Notifications: To send you updates, messages, and alerts.
  • Phone: To enhance the app's functionality and security, such as verifying your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Profile Creation: To set up and personalize your profile.
  • Content Sharing: To allow you to upload images and descriptions within the app.
  • Communication: To send notifications and updates related to your use of the app.
  • Security: To protect your account and our services from unauthorized access.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored in Firestore, a database service provided by Firebase. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. All data collected is used solely for the purposes outlined in this policy.

5. User Rights

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and update your personal information within the app.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us at rajat.garg@wetimeapp.com.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for data collection and processing at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to other websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services, and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our app and updating the “Last Updated” date above.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rajat.garg@wetimeapp.com.
